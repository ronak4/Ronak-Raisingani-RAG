The bill aims to roll back fee adjustments that were implemented on May 1, 2023, which revised the fee charts based on mortgagor credit scores and down payment amounts. By reinstating the previous fee structure, the legislation intends to protect middle-class borrowers from potential increases in mortgage costs.
